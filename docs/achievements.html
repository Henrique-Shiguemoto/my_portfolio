CBIS 2022

Attended the 19th Brazilian Congress of Health Informatics (CBIS), organized by SBIS, to present my Gesture Recognition for Health Information Systems project with the objective of creating a system with the Leap Motion sensor to substitute traditional touch-based interaction systems with computers such as touchpads, mouses and keyboards, this was to prevent the proliferation of disease through touch, specially after the COVID-19 outbreak in Brazil.


This event offered the opportunity to meet leading researchers and demonstrate how computer science research can address real-life challenges in health systems. Thanks to my team’s hard work, we were able to publish our project as a paper.
